## What can I change with the PRO plan?

The PRO plan allows you to change the following things:

- Images for the login screen, placeholders, and logo for the desktop application.
- Desktop application icon
- Texts that explicitly mention the name of Reach on behalf of your organization.
- Images of the Reach Secure Environment (RSE)
